Composite cans

Najmi Industries is a composite can manufacturer based in Bangalore. Having division in Mumbai and Baroda being in business since 1965 Najmi supplies to all major range of Industries throughout the country.

Composite cans find diverse usage in Food, Pharmaceuticals, Oil and Grease, Spices, Talcum powder, Paints, Agro-chemicals, Distilleries etc.

 

Composite cans are generally used for product packagings which are durable, portable and stackable. Composite cans which have extended shelf life helps preserve the freshness of the product. Its vast applications are not only applied for Food product packaging but also for industries such as Agarbattis, Explosives, Perfumes, and Bottles etc…

Composite cans are abundantly used in Industries such as:

    • Insecticides
    • Grease
    • Games
    • Paints
    • Protein supplements
    • Cosmetics
    • Food and confectionery
    • Candles
    • Distilleries
    • Wrist watches
    • Under garments
    • Toys
    • Posters
    • Nuts

     

     

Najmi offers various protective barriers as inner liners, composite can top options, composite can end options and overcap options depending on your product.
