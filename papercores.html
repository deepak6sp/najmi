Paper Cores

Najmi offers paper cores with unique and newest designs which make them superior over other brands. We use the nicest raw materials which make our paper cores appropriate for any kind of usability. The quality paper cores are offered by Najmi Industries can be availed at the least prices anyone can afford.

    • Kraft paper mill
    • Plastic Industries
    • Polyester films
    • PP strap pins
    • Furnishing fabrics
    • Textiles
    • Civil Construction
    • Fabric and Jute winding
    • DTP winding
